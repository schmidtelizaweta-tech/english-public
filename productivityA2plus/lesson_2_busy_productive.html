Block 3
Present Perfect or Past Simple?
9
Today I three meetings so far.
10
I the report to my boss at 4 p.m. yesterday.
11
I any real progress on my project this week.
12
She all her tasks before lunch on Monday.

Remember: Present Perfect connects past actions to now (today, this week, so far). Past Simple is for finished actions at a specific time in the past (yesterday, at 4 p.m., on Monday).
