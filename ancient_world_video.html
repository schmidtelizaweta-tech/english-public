Peer Mission

🏺 Message from the Past

You are preparing a mysterious exhibit about Ancient Egypt.

Choose one object, person, belief or tradition from the topic. Describe it without saying its name.

Your description should include:

  • what it looks like;
  • what it was used for;
  • why it was important;
  • one surprising fact.

Write 4–6 sentences. Another history lover will read your message and try to guess what you described.

💡 Use at least three expressions from the lesson.
